Use analogies to find the equivalents of the following
equations for rotation in a plane:
\begin{align*}
        KE  &=  p^2/2m \\
        \Delta x  &=  v_\zu{o}\Delta t + (1/2)a\Delta t^2\\
        W  &=   F \Delta x
\end{align*}

Example:
$v = \Delta x/\Delta t        \rightarrow        \omega = \Delta\theta/\Delta t$
